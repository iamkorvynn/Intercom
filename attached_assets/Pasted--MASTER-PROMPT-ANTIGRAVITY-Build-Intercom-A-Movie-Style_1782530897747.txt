# PROJECT OVERVIEW

Intercom is **not a messaging app**.

It is **not a walkie-talkie app**.

It is a **persistent private communication system** inspired by movie intercoms.

Two people connect once.

From that moment, they remain connected.

There are:

❌ No phone calls

❌ No ringing

❌ No dialing

❌ No waiting for someone to answer

Instead,

User presses

↓

Voice streams instantly

↓

Partner hears it immediately

↓

Release

↓

Transmission ends

Think of the communication devices used in:

* John Wick
* Mission Impossible
* The Dark Knight
* Military radios
* NASA communications
* Police intercom systems

The experience should feel instant.

# IMPORTANT TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS

* **Android first**. Optimize for Android before iOS because Android allows a closer approximation of an always-connected experience.
* Respect platform limitations. Do **not** attempt unsupported 24/7 background microphone access or violate Android/iOS background execution policies.
* Keep a lightweight persistent connection alive where possible, and activate audio streaming only while the Push-to-Talk button is pressed.
* Design the architecture so future features like group communication and desktop clients can be added without major refactoring.

# SUCCESS CRITERIA

The final product should feel like carrying a **real private intercom** in your pocket. The user opens the app once, pairs with someone, and from then on, communication is as simple as pressing one button to speak—fast, reliable, secure, and with a premium cinematic experience.
